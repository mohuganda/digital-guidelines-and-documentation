# Chapter 19: Nutrition

## 19.1.3 Nutrition in Diabetes

Don’t use products marketed as ”diabetic foods, drinks or
herbs” (they are expensive and of no benefit)

Routine supplementation with vitamins and minerals without underlying actual deficiency is not beneficial, patients
should eat lots of fruits and vegetables e.g, a combination
of selenium and molinga has been proven to be beneficial

Obese and overweight patients need to be encouraged to
reduce weight using exercise and diet modifications
