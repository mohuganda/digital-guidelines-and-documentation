# Chapter 19: Nutrition

This chapter provides guidance on nutrition in special populations, infant and young child feeding, nutrition in HIV/AIDS, nutrition in diabetes, malnutrition, acute malnutrition in children, severe acute malnutrition, treatment of associated conditions, discharge from nutritional programmes, severe acute malnutrition in infants under 6 months, obesity, and overweight.

Use this chapter to support nutritional assessment, classification, treatment planning, counselling, referral, and follow-up.
