# Chapter 5: Respiratory Diseases

## 5.2.3 Coryza (Common Cold)

ICD10 CODE: J00

Acute inflammation of the upper respiratory tract; rhinitis (nasal mucosa)
and rhinopharyngitis (nasal and pharyngitis).
Cause

Viruses - several types, often rhinoviruses

Clinical features

Onset usually sudden

Tickling sensation in nose and sneezing

Throat dry and sore

Profuse nasal watery or purulent discharge, tearing

Complications

Sinusitis

Lower respiratory tract infection (pneumonia)

Ear ache, deafness, otitis media

Headache

Nasal allergy
