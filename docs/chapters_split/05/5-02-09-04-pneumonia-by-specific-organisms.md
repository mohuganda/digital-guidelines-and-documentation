# Chapter 5: Respiratory Diseases

## 5.2.9.4 Pneumonia by Specific Organisms
Management of pneumonia
TREATMENT
Stapylococcus pneumonia
This form is especially common following a recent
influenza infection. It can cause empyema and pneumatocele.
Adults and children >5 years:
 Cloxacillin 1-2 g IV or IM every 6 hours for 10-14 days
Child >5 years: 50 mg/kg per dose (max: 2 g)
Child 2 months-5 years
