# Chapter 5: Respiratory Diseases

## 5.3.2.2 Prevention and Infection Control of TB

Adjunctive treatment

Case diagnosis and management

A TB patient is more infectious before they start TB treatment.

High Priority Patients For Contact Tracing
Bacteriologically confirmed PTB (Smear positive or Xpert
positive or Culture positive)
Cavitation of chest x-ray
Age < 5 years
MDR TB • PLHIV
Other preventive measures
BCG vaccination at birth to prevent severe forms of TB
TB Preventive Treatment for categories at risk
General hygiene
Avoidance of overcrowding
Avoid drinking unboiled milk
