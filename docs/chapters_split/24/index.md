# Chapter 24: Surgery, Radiology and Anaesthesia

This chapter provides guidance on selected surgical conditions, diagnostic imaging, and anaesthesia. It covers intestinal obstruction, internal haemorrhage, management of medical conditions in surgical patients, newborn surgical emergencies, surgical antibiotic prophylaxis, diagnostic imaging from a clinical perspective, and general considerations for anaesthesia.

Use this chapter to support surgical assessment, perioperative planning, imaging decisions, antibiotic prophylaxis, anaesthesia preparation, and referral.
