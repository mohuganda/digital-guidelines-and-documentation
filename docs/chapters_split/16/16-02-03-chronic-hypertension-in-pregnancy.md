# Chapter 16: Obstetric Conditions

## 16.2.3 Chronic Hypertension in Pregnancy
ICD 10 CODE: O10, O13

Blood pressure >140/90 present before the pregnancy or starting
before 20 weeks.
Pregnant women with chronic hypertension should continue to follow
the lifestyle modifications for controlling hypertension such as:

No alcohol

Regular moderate exercise, brisk walking for 30 minutes at
least 3 times a week

Smoking cessation.

Health worker should:

Ask mother about foetal movements at each visit

Aim for BP <140/90 mmHg

Consider labour if BP is persistently ³160/90 mmHg,
pregnancy ³37 weeks gestation, and if there is maternal or
foetal compromise, e.g. poor SFH growth
