## 16.3.2 Vaginal Bleeding in Early Pregnancy/ Abortion
ICD10 CODE: O20

This is almost always abnormal, and patients may need to be admitted
or referred. The most common causes of bleeding in the first six months
(<26 weeks gestation) are abortion and ectopic pregnancy
Abortion (miscarriage) occurs when the foetus is lost before 28 weeks
of pregnancy.
