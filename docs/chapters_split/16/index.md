# Chapter 16: Obstetric Conditions

This chapter covers antenatal care, high-risk pregnancy, common pregnancy complaints, selected conditions in pregnancy, antenatal complications, labour and delivery, acute obstetric complications, newborn care, postpartum conditions, and fetal death. It includes guidance on anaemia, HIV in pregnancy, hypertension, malaria, diabetes, bleeding, pre-eclampsia, eclampsia, obstructed labour, postpartum haemorrhage, puerperal sepsis, and essential newborn care.

Use this chapter to support maternal and newborn care from pregnancy through delivery and the postpartum period.
