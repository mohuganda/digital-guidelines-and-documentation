# Chapter 16: Obstetric Conditions

## 16.2.5 Diabetes in Pregnancy

CHAPTER 16: Obstetric Conditions

ICD10 CODE: O24

Risk factors (and indication for screening)

- BMI >35 kg/m2

- Age >40 years

- GDM in previous pregnancy

- Family history (8 first degree relatives) of diabetes

- Previous unexplained third trimester death, macrosomic

baby (weight >4 kg)

- Polyhydramnios

- Glycosuria

- Foetus large for gestational age
