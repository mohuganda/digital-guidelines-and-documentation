# Chapter 11: Blood Diseases and Blood Transfusion Guidelines

This chapter covers blood disorders and blood transfusion practice. It includes anaemia, iron deficiency anaemia, megaloblastic anaemia, normocytic anaemia, bleeding disorders, sickle cell disease, blood products, indications for transfusion, and adverse transfusion reactions.

Use this chapter to support diagnosis, treatment, transfusion decision-making, monitoring, and referral for blood-related conditions.
