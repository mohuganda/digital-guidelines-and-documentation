# Chapter 1: Emergencies and Trauma

## 1.3.6 Iron Poisoning

ICD10 CODE: T45.4

Common in children, due to the candy-like aspect of iron tablets. Ingestion
of a quantity <40 mg/kg of elemental iron is unlikely to cause problems.
Doses >60 mg/kg can cause serious toxicity.
Note: the common tablet of 200 mg of an iron salt contains 60-65 mg
of elemental iron.
