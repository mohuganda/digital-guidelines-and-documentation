# Chapter 1: Emergencies and Trauma

## 1.3.9 Opioid Poisoning ICD10 CODE: T40

Voluntary or accidental overdose of opioid drugs like codeine,
morphine, heroin used for therapeutic or recreational purposes.
Clinical features

Respiratory depression
Hypotension, hypothermia
Pinpoint pupils
Decreased mental status until coma
