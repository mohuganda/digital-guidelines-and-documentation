# Chapter 1: Emergencies and Trauma

## 1.1.2.1 Hypvovolaemic Shock In Children

Principles of management are similar to the ones in adults BUT:
- Recognising this may be more difficult than in adults
- Vital signs may change little, even when up to 25% of blood
volume is lost (class 1 and 2 hypovolaemia)

- Tachycardia is often the first response to hypovolaemia but may

also be caused by fear or pain
