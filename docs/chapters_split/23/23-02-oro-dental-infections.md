# Chapter 23: Oral and Dental Conditions

## 23.2 ORO-DENTAL INFECTIONS

CHAPTER 23 : Oral and Dental Conditions

TREATMENT

23.2.1. Prevention of Dental Caries and Other Conditions Due to
Poor Oral lannin

Advise patient to reduce sugary foods and soft drinks, and to
have adequate fresh fruit and vegetables in their diet

Advise patient to brush their teeth at least twice a day (morning and evening) or preferably after every meal (wait at least 30
minutes if you have consumed acidic food like lemon, oranges,
grapes)

Dental flossing at least once a day

Tooth strengthening and protection by rinsing with fluoride rinses and applying sealants to susceptible sites on teeth

Prevention and early management of dental caries

Advise patient to have a dental check-up every six months

Good nutrition
