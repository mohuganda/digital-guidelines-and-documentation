pedunculated lump, usually located on the palate
or lateral surface of the
tongue

characteristic

Saliva extravasation into the tissues
from damage to minor salivary gland
ducts. They are commonly seen in
the lower labial and ventral lingual
mucosa
Ranula

A mucocele that occurs from the
sublingual gland
