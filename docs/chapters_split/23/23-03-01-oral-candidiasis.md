# Chapter 23: Oral and Dental Conditions

## 23.3.1 Oral Candidiasis

ICD10 CODE: B37.0

Cause

- Caused primarily by Candida albicans

Clinical features

- Common in immunosuppressed, infants, and after prolonged

antibiotic treatment

- In advanced HIV it can present as intractable oral and oesopha-

geal candidiasis. Angular cheilitis is also common Management
