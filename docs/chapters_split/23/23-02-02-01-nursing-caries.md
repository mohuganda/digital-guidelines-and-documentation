# Chapter 23: Oral and Dental Conditions

## 23.2.2.1 Nursing Caries

These are anterior caries in the pre-school child, due to prolonged and
improper feeding habits.

TREATMENT

Causes

Clinical features

Rapid progression of decay commencing labially and quickly encircling the teeth

Teeth are affected in order of eruption

Lower incisors are rarely affected as they are protected by the
tongue during suckling and directly cleansed by secretions from
sublingual and submandibular salivary glands
