# Chapter 23: Oral and Dental Conditions

## 23.2.2.1 Nursing Caries

Causes

- Frequent and prolonged consumption of fluid containing fer-

mentable carbohydrates from a bottle, feeder cup, or on- demand nightly breast feeding after 15 months of age

Clinical features

- Rapid progression of decay commencing labially and quickly en-

circling the teeth

- Teeth are affected in order of eruption

- Lower incisors are rarely affected as they are protected by the

tongue during suckling and directly cleansed by secretions from sublingual and submandibular salivary glands

############ Management

|TREATMENT|LOC|
|---|---|
