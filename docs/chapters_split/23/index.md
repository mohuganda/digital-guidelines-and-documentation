# Chapter 23: Oral and Dental Conditions

This chapter covers common oral and dental conditions including halitosis, dentin hypersensitivity, malocclusion, fluorosis, dental caries, pulpitis, dental abscess, gingivitis, necrotizing ulcerative gingivitis, periodontitis, stomatitis, aphthous ulceration, pericoronitis, osteomyelitis of the jaw, HIV-associated oral conditions, oral trauma, and oral tumours.

Use this chapter to support oral health assessment, pain and infection management, prevention counselling, and timely dental or specialist referral.
