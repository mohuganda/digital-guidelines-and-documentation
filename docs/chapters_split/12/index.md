# Chapter 12: Oncology

This chapter introduces common cancers, groups at increased risk, early signs and symptoms, urgent warning signs, cancer prevention, screening, and common cancers in children and adults. It emphasizes early recognition and timely referral.

Use this chapter to support cancer suspicion, patient counselling, prevention, early detection, and referral into appropriate oncology services.
