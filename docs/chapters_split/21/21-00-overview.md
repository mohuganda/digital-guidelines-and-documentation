Fungal infection usually confined to the mucous membranes and external
layers of skin. Severe forms are usually associated with immunosuppressive
conditions, such as HIV/AIDS, diabetes, pregnancy, cancer, prolonged
antibiotic use, and steroids.

Clinical features

Blockage, FB may be seen

Noise in the ear if it is a live FB like an insect

Hearing loss

If attempts have been made to remove the FB:

Bleeding/discharge from the ear
