# Chapter 21: Ear, Nose and Throat Conditions

This chapter provides guidance on common ear, nose, and throat conditions. It covers foreign bodies, wax in the ear, otitis externa, otitis media, glue ear, mastoiditis, epistaxis, nasal allergy, sinusitis, atrophic rhinitis, adenoid disease, airway foreign body, food passage foreign body, sore throat, tonsillitis, and peritonsillar abscess.

Use this chapter to guide ENT assessment, initial treatment, counselling, and referral for potentially serious airway, ear, nasal, and throat conditions.
