# Chapter 6: Gastrointestinal and Hepatic Diseases

This chapter provides guidance on gastrointestinal emergencies, gastrointestinal infections, digestive disorders, anorectal disorders, and hepatic and biliary diseases. It includes conditions such as appendicitis, acute pancreatitis, gastrointestinal bleeding, peritonitis, diarrhoea, amoebiasis, dysentery, dysphagia, dyspepsia, reflux disease, gastritis, peptic ulcer disease, constipation, haemorrhoids, hepatitis, cirrhosis, ascites, jaundice, gallstones, and cholecystitis.

Use this chapter to guide clinical assessment, supportive care, treatment, and referral for gastrointestinal and liver-related conditions.
