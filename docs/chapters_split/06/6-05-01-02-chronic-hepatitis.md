## 6.5.1.2 Chronic Hepatitis

CHAPTER 6: Gastrointestinal and Hepatic Diseases

Caution

Management

ICD10 CODE: B18

The hepatitis viruses B, C and D can give chronic infection
with chronic low level inflammation of the liver and progressive
damage which may progress to liver cirrhosis.
