## 6.5.1.1 Acute Hepatitis ICD10 CODES: B15, B16, B17, B19
Clinical features

Asymptomatic

Classic form: fever, fatigue, malaise, abdominal discomfort
(right upper quadrant), nausea, diarrhoea, anorexia, followed by jaundice, dark urine and more or less clay coloured stool

Fulminant form: acute liver failure due to massive liver necrosis, often fatal. It is more common in HepB patients
with secondary infection with D virus and pregnant women
who get hepatitis E in their third trimester
