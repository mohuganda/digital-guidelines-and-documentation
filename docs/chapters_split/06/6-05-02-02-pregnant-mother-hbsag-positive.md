## 6.5.2.2 Pregnant Mother HbsAg Positive

If a pregnant mother is found HBsAg positive:

If also HIV positive,

- Start ARVs.

eventual need of antiretrovirals
- Child should be immunized at birth

Breastfeeding is safe

6.5.4

Liver Cirrhosis

RR

CICD10 CODES: K74, K70.3

Cirrhosis is a chronic disease with necrosis of liver cells followed by
fibrosis and nodule formation. Decompensated cirrhosis is defined by
the presence of complications such as ascites, variceal bleeding, encephalopathy, or jaundice which result from the portal hypertension and liver
insufficiency caused by cirrhosis.
