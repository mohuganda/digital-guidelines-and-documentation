## 6.5.4.3 Hepatic Encephalopathy (HE)

ICD10 CODES: 70.41, 71.11, 72.11, 72.91
Hepatic encephalopathy is a syndrome of neuropsychiatric symptoms and
signs, including coma, observed in patients with cirrhosis. It is probably
due to the accumulation of toxins in the blood.
