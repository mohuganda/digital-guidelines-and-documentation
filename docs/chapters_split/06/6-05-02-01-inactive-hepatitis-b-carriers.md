# Chapter 6: Gastrointestinal and Hepatic Diseases

## 6.5.2.1 Inactive Hepatitis B Carriers

ICD10 CODE: B18.1

Carriers are patients with chronic but inactive infection:
- HBsAg positive for more than 6 months plus
- Persistently normal liver function (at least 3 times in 12
months) and
- No evidence of viral replication (negative HBeAg and/or
- HBV DNA < 2000 IU/ml)
Patients classified as inactive carriers need to be monitored once a year
with CBC, renal and liver tests, HBsAg, abdominal ultrasound. If possible,
do HBV-DNA every 3 years.
They are not highly infectious but close contacts should be immunized
and appropriate precautions should be followed.
