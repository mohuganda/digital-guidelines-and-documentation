# Chapter 18: Immunization

This chapter covers routine childhood vaccination, the national immunization schedule, hepatitis B vaccination, yellow fever vaccination, tetanus prevention, neonatal tetanus prophylaxis, and vaccination against COVID-19.

Use this chapter to guide immunization service delivery, catch-up decisions, prevention counselling, and vaccine-related public health practice.
