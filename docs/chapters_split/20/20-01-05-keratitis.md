# Chapter 20: Eye Conditions

## 20.1.5 Keratitis

ICD10 CODE: H16

Prevention

Inflammation of the cornea.
Causes
Infection: Bacterial, viral, or fungal; leading to corneal ulceration

Trauma: Chemical, foreign bodies

Clinical features

Redness and tearing

Fear of light

Cornea is not clear and will stain with fluorescein in the
case of corneal ulcer (pattern of staining depends on the
causative agent, for example dendritic in viral keratitis)

Visual acuity is usually reduced

Condition is often unilateral

The eye is painful
