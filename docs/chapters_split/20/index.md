# Chapter 20: Eye Conditions

This chapter covers infectious, inflammatory, traumatic, visual, and tumour-related eye conditions. It includes conjunctivitis, stye, trachoma, keratitis, uveitis, orbital cellulitis, endophthalmitis, xerophthalmia, cataract, glaucoma, diabetic retinopathy, refractive errors, low vision, foreign body, ocular injuries, chemical injuries, retinoblastoma, and conjunctival carcinoma.

Use this chapter to support eye assessment, treatment, prevention of complications, urgent referral, and protection of vision.
