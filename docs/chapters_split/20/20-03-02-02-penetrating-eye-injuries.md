Chemical Injuries to the Eye

ICD10 CODE: S05.8

Various chemicals may injure the eye when they come into contact
with the eyes or face. The commonest are acidic or alkaline chemical
products.
Acids and Alkaline products will cause serious injuries to the lids,
cornea, and conjunctivae.
