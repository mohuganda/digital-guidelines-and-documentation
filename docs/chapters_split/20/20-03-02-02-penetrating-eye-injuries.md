|Corneal and Scleral Perforations<br><br>All perforations of the cornea or sclera are serious injuries and may lead to blindness.<br><br> Apply an eye shield to protect the eye, give a pain reliever and refer the patient immediately to an Ophthalmologist<br><br> At the secondary or tertiary level the treatment of corneal/scleral lacerations is immediate repair with 10/0 sutures under an operating microscope, or if the laceration is extensive, an immediate evisceration of the eye should be performed|HC2<br><br>RR|
