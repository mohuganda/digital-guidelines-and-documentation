# Chapter 13: Palliative Care

This chapter provides guidance on pain assessment, nociceptive and neuropathic pain management, pain management in adults and children, and symptom control in palliative care. It also covers breathlessness, nausea and vomiting, pressure ulcers, fungating wounds, anorexia, cachexia, hiccups, dry or painful mouth, other symptoms, and end-of-life care.

Use this chapter to support comfort, dignity, symptom relief, family counselling, and continuity of care for patients with life-limiting illness.
