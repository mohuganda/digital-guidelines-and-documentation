## 13.1.2 Nociceptive Pain Management
There are two goals of pain management:

There are two goals of pain management:

- Diagnose and treat the disease causing the pain

- Achieve total pain relief with minimal side-effects and en-

able the patient to live as normal a life as possible

- Pain can be treated through use of medicines and/or nondrug treatment
