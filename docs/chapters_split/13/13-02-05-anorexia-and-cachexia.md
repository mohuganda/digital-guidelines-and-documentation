# Chapter 13: Palliative Care

## 13.2.5 Anorexia and Cachexia

ICD10 CODE: R63.0 AND R64

Causes

- Nausea and vomiting, constipation, gastrointestinal ob-

struction

- Sore mouth, mouth tumours, malodour

- Hypercalcaemia, hyponatraemia, uraemia, liver failure

- Medications

- Depression
