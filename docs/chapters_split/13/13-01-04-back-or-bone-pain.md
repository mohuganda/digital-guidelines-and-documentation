# Chapter 13: Palliative Care

## 13.1.4 Back or Bone Pain

Includes pain in the lumbar region of the spine or bone pain anywhere within the body. Causes Potential causes of back or bone pain:

- Disc degeneration (often has a neuropathic element be-

cause of pressure on sciatic or other nerve)

- Osteoporosis (if collapse of vertebrae or fracture) Infection (e.g. TB, brucellosis, PID, retroperitoneal)

- Metastatic cancers, renal disease

- Strain

- Congenital abnormalities

- Spondylolisthesis

Clinical Features Each situation will differ depending on the cause of the pain

- If an infection is present: throbbing and constant pain

- If sciatica, sciatic nerve roots will be involved
