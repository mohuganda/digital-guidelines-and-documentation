# Chapter 13: Palliative Care

## 13.2.6 Hiccup

Repeated involuntary spasmodic diaphragmatic and inspiratory intercostal muscle contractions. Hiccups up to 48 hours are acute, those lasting more than 48 hours are persistent and more than 2 months are intractable.

Causes

- Gastric distension, GERD, gastritis, diaphragmatic irrita-

tion by supraphrenic metastasis, phrenic nerve irritation

- Metabolic: uraemia, hypokalaemia, hypocalcaemia, hyper-

glycaemia, hypocapnia

- Infection: oesophageal candidiasis

- Brain tumour, stroke, stress Management
