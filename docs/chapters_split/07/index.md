# Chapter 7: Renal and Urinary Diseases

This chapter covers renal and urological conditions including acute renal failure, chronic kidney disease, safe use of medicines in renal failure, glomerulonephritis, nephrotic syndrome, cystitis, pyelonephritis, prostatitis, renal colic, benign prostatic hyperplasia, bladder outlet obstruction, and urinary incontinence.

Use this chapter to support recognition, safe prescribing, treatment initiation, monitoring, and referral for kidney and urinary tract disease.
