# Chapter 14: Gynecological Conditions

This chapter covers common gynecological conditions including dysmenorrhoea, pelvic inflammatory disease, abnormal uterine bleeding, and menopause. It supports assessment and treatment of reproductive health conditions outside pregnancy.

Use this chapter to guide history taking, examination, treatment, counselling, and referral for gynecological complaints.
