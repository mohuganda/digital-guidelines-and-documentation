# Chapter 22: Skin Diseases

## 22.5.2 Urticaria/Papular Urticari

ICD10 CODE: L50

- Endogenous: familial, also associated with other allergic diseases

- Exogenous: agents include sunlight, chemicals, certain foods,

insect bites Clinical features

- Inflammation of skin: transient itching hives and wheals

- Papular urticaria: vesicles, redness, oedema, oozing in case of

insect bites

Differential diagnosis

- Fungal and bacterial infections of the skin

- Helminth infestations Investigations  No satisfactory investigations for skin allergy  Blood: haemogram to demonstrate eosinophilia  Stool: microscopy to exclude worms
