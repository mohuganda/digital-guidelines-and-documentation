# Chapter 22: Skin Diseases

## 22.1.2 Boils (Furuncle)/Carbuncle

ICD CODE: L02

A boil or furuncle is a deep-seated infection of the hair follicles with a
walled-off collection of pus. A carbuncle is a cluster of interconnected
furuncles.
Cause

Bacterial infection with Staphylococcus aureus, leading to to the
collection of pus
