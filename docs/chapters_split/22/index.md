# Chapter 22: Skin Diseases

This chapter covers bacterial, viral, fungal, parasitic, inflammatory, allergic, ulcerative, chronic wound, drug-induced, and congenital skin conditions. It includes impetigo, boils, cellulitis, herpes simplex, herpes zoster, fungal infections, scabies, lice, jiggers, acne, urticaria, eczema, psoriasis, leg ulcers, Stevens-Johnson syndrome, toxic epidermal necrolysis, and congenital skin disorders.

Use this chapter to support skin assessment, infection control, treatment selection, wound care, counselling, and referral for severe skin reactions.
