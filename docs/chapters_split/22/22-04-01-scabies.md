# Chapter 22: Skin Diseases

## 22.4.1 Scabies

ICD10 CODE: B86

Contagious skin disease associated with severe itch
Cause

A parasitic mite, Sarcopterus scabiei hominis

Transmitted by direct skin contact with infected person

Clinical features

Intense itching, especially at night

Wheals, papules, vesicles, and thread-like burrows



Common in flexural areas, i.e., wrists and inter-digital creases,
axillae, nipples, buttocks, and genitalia

Scratching spreads mites to other areas leading to widespread,
intensely pruritic eruption

Secondary infection is common

Differential diagnosis

Papular urticaria, atopic or seborrhoeic dermatitis

Drug eruptions

Onchocerciasis

Contact dermatitis
