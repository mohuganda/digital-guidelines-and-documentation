# Chapter 15: Family Planning (FP)

## 15.2.9 Natural FP: Cervical Mucus Method (CMM) and Moon
Beads ICD10 CODE: Z30.02

CMM is a fertility awareness-based method of FP which relies on the
change in the nature of vaginal mucus during the menstrual cycle in order
to detect the fertile time. During this time, the couple avoids pregnancy by
changing sexual behaviour as follows:

Abstaining from sexual intercourse: Avoiding vaginal sex
completely (also called periodic abstinence)

Using barriers methods, e.g., condoms, cervical caps

Guidance on correct use of the method is only available at
centres with specially trained service providers.
