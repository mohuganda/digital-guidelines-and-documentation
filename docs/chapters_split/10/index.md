# Chapter 10: Musculoskeletal and Joint Diseases

This chapter provides guidance on infections and inflammatory or degenerative conditions affecting bones, joints, and muscles. It covers pyogenic arthritis, osteomyelitis, pyomyositis, tuberculosis of the spine, rheumatoid arthritis, gout arthritis, and osteoarthritis.

Use this chapter to guide assessment, pain control, infection management, functional support, treatment, and referral for musculoskeletal and joint conditions.
