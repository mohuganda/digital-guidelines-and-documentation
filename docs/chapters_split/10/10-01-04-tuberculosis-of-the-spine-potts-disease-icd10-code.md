# Chapter 10: Musculoskeletal andJoint Diseases

## 10.1.4 Tuberculosis of the Spine (Potts Disease) ICD10 CODE:
A18.01

Tuberculous spondylitis (Pott’s disease) is the most common form of
skeletal TB; it usually affects the lower thoracic and upper lumbar region.
Infection begins with inflammation
of the intervertebral joints and can spread to involve the adjacent vertebral
body. Once two adjacent vertebrae are involved, infection can involve
the adjoining intervertebral disc space, leading to vertebral collapse.
Subsequent kyphosis can lead to cord compression and paraplegia.
