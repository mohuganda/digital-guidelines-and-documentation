# Chapter 8: Endocrine and Metabolic Diseases

This chapter provides guidance on endocrine and metabolic conditions including Addison’s disease, Cushing’s syndrome, diabetes mellitus, diabetic ketoacidosis, hyperosmolar hyperglycaemic state, goitre, hyperthyroidism, hypothyroidism, and central precocious puberty.

Use this chapter to guide diagnosis, emergency recognition, long-term management, monitoring, counselling, and referral for endocrine and metabolic disorders.
