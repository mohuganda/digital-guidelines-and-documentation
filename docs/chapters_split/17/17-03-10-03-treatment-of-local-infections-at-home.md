|INFECTION|TREATMENT|
|---|---|
|Eye infection| Clean both eyes 4 times daily:<br><br>- Wash hands<br>- Ask child to close eyes<br>- Use clean cloth with clean water to gently remove pus<br>- Use a different part of the cloth for each eye<br>- Clean each eye from nose-side to ear-side to avoid passing the infection from one eye to the other<br><br><br> Apply tetracycline eye ointment 1% to each eye 4 times daily after cleaning the eyes<br><br>- Ask the child to look up<br>- Squirt a small amount (5 mm length) on the inside of the lower eyelid<br>- Wash hands again<br><br><br> Continue application until the redness has disappeared<br><br> Do not put anything else into the eye|
