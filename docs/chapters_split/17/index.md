# Chapter 17: Childhood Illness

This chapter provides guidance on assessment and management of sick newborns, young infants up to 2 months, and children aged 2 months to 5 years. It covers danger signs, local infection, jaundice, diarrhoea, dehydration, HIV exposure, feeding problems, immunization, malnutrition, anaemia, counselling, community case management, and growth standards.

Use this chapter to support IMNCI-style assessment, treatment, counselling, referral, and follow-up for newborns and children.
