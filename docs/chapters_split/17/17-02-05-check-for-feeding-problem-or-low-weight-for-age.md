# Chapter 17: Childhood Illness

## 17.2.5 Check for Feeding Problem or Low Weight-for-Age

Observe breastfeeding for 4 minutes: is the infant able to
attach properly to the breast? For good attachment, the
following should be present:

Chin touching breast
Mouth wide open
Lower lip turned outwards
More areola visible above than below the mouth

Is the infant able to suckle effectively? This means slow,
deep sucks with occasional pauses

Clear a blocked nose if it interferes with breastfeeding
