# Chapter 4: Cardiovascular Diseases

This chapter provides guidance on common cardiovascular conditions, including deep vein thrombosis, pulmonary embolism, infective endocarditis, heart failure, pulmonary oedema, atrial fibrillation, hypertension, hypertensive emergencies, ischaemic heart disease, pericarditis, rheumatic fever, rheumatic heart disease, and stroke.

Use this chapter to support cardiovascular assessment, risk recognition, treatment initiation, follow-up, and referral.
