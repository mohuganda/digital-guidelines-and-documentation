# Chapter 3: HIV/AIDS and Sexually Transmitted Infections

## 3.1.11 Prevention of HIV
Behavioural change

Always follow safe sex practices (e.g. use condoms; avoid
multiple sexual partners)

Never share used needles, syringes, razors, hair shavers,
nail cutters, and other sharp objects

Avoid tattooing, body-piercing, and scarification unless
carried out under strictly hygienic conditions in properly
controlled premises

Delay start of sexual activity in adolescence

Discourage cross generational and transactional sex

Avoid violence and abuse

Biomedical prevention interventions
PMTCT

Safe Male Circumcision

ART with viral suppression

PEP (Post Exposure Prophylaxis)

PrEP (Pre Exposure Prophylaxis)

Blood transfusion safety

STI screening and treatment

Safe infusion and injection practices

Adherence to infection control procedures
