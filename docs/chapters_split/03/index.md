# Chapter 3: HIV/AIDS and Sexually Transmitted Infections

Always refer to the latest Ministry of Health PMTCT, ART, and STI guidelines for the management of HIV and sexually transmitted infections. This chapter is adapted from Uganda’s consolidated guidance for prevention and treatment of HIV and provides practical direction for HIV diagnosis, clinical staging, ART, monitoring, ARV toxicity, prevention, PMTCT, HIV-exposed infant care, opportunistic infections, and STI syndromes.

Use this chapter to support HIV and STI service delivery, continuity of care, treatment monitoring, prevention, and management of related complications.
