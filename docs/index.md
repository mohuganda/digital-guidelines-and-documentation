# Ministry of Health Digital Knowledge Platform

A centralized digital platform for accessing Ministry of Health publications, guidelines, manuals, reports, bulletins, and reference materials in a structured and searchable format.

The platform transforms static documents into usable digital resources to support service delivery, planning, learning, supervision, and decision-making. It is being developed in phases, beginning with selected Ministry resources and expanding progressively across multiple thematic areas.

## Platform capabilities

The platform is designed to improve how Ministry information is accessed and used:

- structured navigation across large documents
- quick access to specific sections within publications
- organized grouping across multiple thematic areas
- scalable support for hundreds of documents
- foundation for intelligent search and assisted information retrieval

## Implementation approach

The current phase focuses on proving that Ministry documents can be converted from static files into structured, searchable web content. The same approach can later be applied to additional PDFs, reports, bulletins, manuals, and reference documents from the Ministry library.

The platform will support three levels of use:

- **Browse:** users navigate content by thematic area and document structure.
- **Search:** users find terms, sections, and related guidance faster than in static PDFs.
- **Ask:** users query approved content through an AI-assisted interface with references back to source documents.

## Important notes

!!! note "Use of content"
    This platform provides a digital representation of Ministry of Health publications to improve accessibility and usability. It does not replace official documents, approval processes, or formal dissemination channels.

!!! note "Future development"
    The platform will continue to expand in both content and functionality, including improved document coverage, better mobile presentation, and intelligent assistance to support information discovery and use.
