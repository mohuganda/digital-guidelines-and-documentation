    H

    TREATMENT

    Differential diagnosis

    Cellulitis, boil

    Osteomyelitis

    Peritonitis (in pyomyositis of abdominal muscles)

    Investigations  Blood: Full blood count  Pus: culture and sensitivity  Consider HIV infection TREATMENT

    LOC

    

    Elevate and immobilise affected limb

    HC3

    

    Cloxacillin 500 mg IV or oral every 6 hours for 5-10 days Child: 12.5-25 mg/kg per dose

     CHAPTER 10: Musculoskeletal andJoint Diseases
